Cricket

The game we all love to play and watch has just gotten pleasant and enjoyable! Presenting the Cricket game where you can be at the top of the leaderboard by competing with online players.

How to Play

Mechanics:

1. Leaderboard: A comprehensive display showcasing each player's name, accompanied by their respective run count and wickets. Keep a keen eye on this board to track your progress and assess your competition's performance

2. Swipe Mechanics: Master the art of the swipe to control your batsman's shot. Timing is critical here; a well-executed swipe at the precise moment ensures maximum run potential.

3. Run Tracking: Each successful shot updates the current score dynamically. The Run Sheet displays where the ball has been hit, adding it to the ongoing score. This real-time feedback allows players to strategize their shots and aim for high-scoring regions on the pitch.

4. Timer: An ever-ticking clock that measures the remaining duration of the match. Utilize your time wisely, as every second counts in your pursuit of victory. A ball will be played every 10 seconds, adding urgency and rhythm to the gameplay.

5. Balls Remaining: Stay informed of the number of balls left in the over, as each match consists of six balls. Strategize accordingly, aiming to make the most of every opportunity at the crease.

Player Tips:

1. Master Timing: Hone your timing skills to perfection, ensuring that each swipe connects with the ball at the optimal moment for maximum impact.

2. Monitor the Leaderboard: Maintain a vigilant watch over the leaderboard, analyzing your position relative to your competitors and strategizing accordingly.
