How to Play

Welcome to the classic game of Snake and Ladders, reimagined for two players in a digital format. Embark on a journey from 0 to 20, where luck and strategy intertwine in a battle for victory. Navigate the game board, to reach the coveted 20th position before your opponent.

Mechanics:

1.Game Board: A grid representing the journey from 0 to 20. Each square signifies a step closer to victory or a setback along the way.

2.Turn-based Gameplay: Players take turns rolling the dice, with a timer dictating the duration of each turn. The "Your Turn" logo alternates between players, signaling whose turn it is to roll the dice and make their move.

3. Dice Rolling: Click the dice icon on your turn to roll it, revealing the number that determines how many steps you advance on the board. Strategize your moves wisely, considering the current board layout and your opponent's position.

4. Victory Conditions: The game concludes when one player reaches the 20th position on the board or holds a higher position than their opponent. The first to achieve these conditions is declared the winner.

Player Tips:

1.Strategic Planning: Develop a game plan based on the current board layout and your opponent's position. Anticipate potential challenges and opportunities presented by snakes and ladders to maximize your chances of success.

2. Turn Management: Utilize the timer to your advantage, making calculated moves within the allotted time frame. Stay focused and avoid rushing decisions, as strategic timing can significantly impact your gameplay.
